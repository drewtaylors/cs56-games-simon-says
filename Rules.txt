*The rules for the game is simple.
*There will be some buttons on the screen.
*When you start the game, buttons will start flashing in certain sequence.
*When it is your turn,you should click on the buttons sequentially in the same way the buttons previously flashed.
*If you get the entire sequence correct, a new round will start but with a new button flash(random generated) added to the end of the previous sequence.
*If your guess is incorrect OR you have got the entire sequence correct, the game ends immediately
*Your score of how many rounds you have successfully guessed is recorded.
*Try your best to get high score!
